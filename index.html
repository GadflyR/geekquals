Equation Puzzle

- Unary operators (`√`, `!`) must go on numbers.
- Binary operators (`+`, `-`, `×`, `÷`, `^`, `(`, `)`), and `=` go in slots.
- `=` starts in the middle and cannot be removed, but can be moved.
If exactly one `=` and correct, shows "Success!"
If invalid, "Invalid expression!"
+ - × ÷ ^ ( ) = √ !
